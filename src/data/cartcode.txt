
                               NOS CARTOGRAPHIC CODES
  
                THIS FILE LISTS CARTOGRAPHIC CODES USED IN DIGITAL HYDROGRAPHIC 
           SURVEY DATA BY THE NATIONAL OCEAN SERVICE (NOS HYDROGRAPHIC
           SURVEY GUIDELINES NO. 35, OCTOBER 31, 1984).  CODES USED IN THE PAST 
           AND NOT REFERENCED BY THE CURRENT GUIDELINES ARE MARKED '[ARCHAIC!'. 
           PRECEDING EACH CODE IS A LETTER THAT INDICATES WHETHER OR NOT IT IS  
           INCLUDED IN THE NOS HYDROGRAPHIC DATA BASE MAINTAINED AT THE
           NATIONAL GEOPHYSICAL DATA CENTER:
  
                S = DEPTH SOUNDING OR ELEVATION (INCLUDED)
                D = FIXED NAVIGATIONAL HAZARD OR AID (INCLUDED)
                B = BOTTOM CHARACTERISTIC (INCLUDED)
                T = TEMPORARY OR NOT-FIXED POINT (NOT INCLUDED)
                L = LINEAR DATA (NOT INCLUDED)
